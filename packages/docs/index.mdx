## What this fork does

BONUS Open Room adds a structured `.bonus/` workspace and separated agent lanes for clean-room work:

- The analysis lane can inspect allowed source materials and produce specs, conformance assets, provenance, and notes.
- The clean-room lane is blocked from `.bonus/source/` and writes fresh implementation, tests, and docs from the spec.
